Bluenet Schedule Format Prettier

How to use

  1. Go to the main "New/Current Students" menu on Bluenet
  2. Click on "My Class Schedule" and select the proper term
  3. Once you see your schedule, right click and select "View Page Source"
  4. Select-All on this page (Ctrl+A) and copy (Ctrl+C) paste (Ctrl+V) it into the text area on this page.
  5. Click Submit to get a visual version of your schedule.
  6. You can now print-screen or print this webpage.
